# Privacy Policy for Sentio

**Last Updated: April 27, 2026**

## Overview

Sentio is a browser extension for Twitch channel pages. It shows an inline viewer counter, opens a popup, and provides a dashboard to help you review viewer patterns and investigate unusual activity. This Privacy Policy explains what information Sentio handles, how it is used, and what choices you have.

## Information Sentio Handles

### 1. Settings and extension state

Sentio stores limited information in Chrome extension storage so the extension can function correctly. This may include:

- your local extension settings and preferences
- dashboard state and related extension state needed to keep the UI working
- temporary data needed to coordinate the popup, dashboard, and Twitch page experience
- limited local diagnostic information

This data is used only to operate Sentio on your device.

### 2. Twitch page and viewer-related data

When you actively use Sentio on a Twitch channel page, the extension reads and processes information available from Twitch pages and Twitch-provided responses needed for its features. Depending on the feature in use, this may include:

- Twitch usernames
- viewer counts and sampled viewer totals
- viewer labels and derived risk signals shown in the UI
- account-age or profile signals exposed through Twitch data available to the extension
- channel-page context needed to detect the current Twitch channel

This information is used to display the inline counter, popup, dashboard, charts, filters, and investigation labels inside the extension.

### 3. Information Sentio does not collect

Sentio does not collect or transmit:

- your browsing history
- data from non-Twitch websites
- passwords, payment information, or unrelated form input
- analytics or telemetry for Sentio-operated servers

Sentio does not run its own backend service for storing your extension data.

## How Sentio Uses Information

Sentio uses the information it handles only to:

- activate on Twitch channel pages
- show its in-page counter and popup
- open and operate the Sentio dashboard
- remember local settings and extension state
- calculate and display viewer-analysis signals within the extension

We do not sell your data, rent your data, or use it for advertising.

## Permissions and Why They Are Needed

Sentio requests only the permissions needed for its Twitch functionality:

- **`storage`**: used to save local settings and extension state
- **`tabs`**: used to open or focus the Sentio dashboard and coordinate with the relevant Twitch tab
- **`*://*.twitch.tv/*`**: used so Sentio can run on Twitch pages where the extension is intended to work

## Data Transmission

Sentio operates in the browser and only interacts with Twitch resources needed for its Twitch-page functionality.

Sentio does not send your extension data to servers operated by us. It also does not use third-party analytics, advertising technology, or remote telemetry services.

## Storage, Retention, and Deletion

- Sentio stores limited settings and extension state locally in Chrome extension storage on your device.
- Viewer-analysis data shown during your use of the extension is handled locally for the Sentio experience.
- You can remove locally stored Sentio data by uninstalling the extension or clearing the extension's stored data in Chrome.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your extension data to advertisers, data brokers, or unrelated third parties.

To the extent Sentio relies on Twitch pages and Twitch-provided data while operating on Twitch, Twitch may process requests according to Twitch's own systems and policies.

## Third-Party Services

Sentio is designed to work with Twitch pages. When you use Sentio, Twitch may process information according to Twitch's own terms and privacy practices.

Twitch Privacy Notice: https://www.twitch.tv/p/legal/privacy-notice/

## Children's Privacy

Sentio is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly design it for use by children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

## Contact

If you have a question about this Privacy Policy or Sentio's data practices, you can:

- open an issue on the GitHub repository: https://github.com/Mallowy-tv/SentinelTracker/issues
- contact us through the browser extension listing support channel
